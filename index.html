Education

NACKADEMIN provides professional higher education services. The Company operates A-level education and advanced vocational education in the areas of construction, design, IT, business, and health

Nackademin AB is located in Stockholm, Sweden and is part of the Colleges & Universities Industry. It is a higher vocational school that offers college education for individuals, as well as education tailored for companies.

Nackademin currently educate almost 2,000 students in various fields and have extensive experience of courses and educations based on the needs of the business community.

Areas include construction, IT, communication, electrical engineering, healthcare and design. This year’s statistics show that nearly 90% of Nackademin students have jobs after graduation.
